About makhor

makhor is a minimal link aggregator inspired by Lobste.rs and Hacker News.

Features

  • Passwordless login via email
  • Invite-only registration with visible invite tree
  • Tagged posts with multiple categories
  • Threaded comments
  • Hats for verified organizational affiliation
  • Full action log for transparency
  • RSS feeds for posts, tags, and users
  • No JavaScript required

RSS Feeds

  • Latest posts
  • Recent comments
  • /rss/tag/NAME.xml - Posts by tag
  • /rss/user/USERNAME.xml - Posts by user

Hats

A hat is a verified identity that lets you speak officially for an organization. When commenting with a hat, your organizational role is displayed. Hats are granted by admins after verification.

Design Philosophy

This site is intentionally minimal:

  • No JavaScript - works in any browser
  • Minimal CSS - fast load times
  • High contrast - easy to read
  • No tracking - respects privacy
  • Pure Go backend - no dependencies
